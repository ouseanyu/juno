Vision

Our vision at AIMSKY is to provide innovative and comfortable home appliances for our clients by providing competitive and environmental friendly products with the best quality

COMPANY INTRODUCTION

AIMSKY started operation since 2014 and registered its trademark AIMCARE in March, 2015. It has 3 main offices-- the headquarters in Washington state, warehouse& sales office in Oregon of America and one branch in Guangzhou city of China which controls product screening and shipping. AIMSKY focuses on electronic home appliances, and our main products are baby monitor and aroma diffuser.
